TIME-WEATHER site is a very helpful website in helping any user to get to know the current temperatures, weather and time-zone of any city entered by them.

STEPS to follow -

  • Write the cityname in the textbox
  • To get the weather update of the city mentioned click the "GET WEATHER" button
  • Then click the "GET TIME-ZONE" button to know the current time-zone of the city
